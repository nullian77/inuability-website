Who We Are

Creating Pathways to Dignity & Independence

Founded by Nancy and Phill, InuAbility was born from a shared commitment to disability inclusion, accessibility, rehabilitation awareness, and independent living support.

Drawing on extensive experience in disability support, aged care, community services and transitional care — InuAbility bridges the gap between individuals, families, employers and communities, creating pathways to true inclusion across Africa.
