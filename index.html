Who We Are

Creating Pathways to Dignity & Independence

Founded by Nancy and Phill, InuAbility was born from a shared commitment to disability inclusion, accessibility, rehabilitation awareness, and independent living support.

Drawing on extensive experience in disability support, aged care, community services and transitional care — InuAbility bridges the gap between individuals, families, healthcare professionals, employers and support systems.

Why It Matters
Accessibility is not a privilege. It is a right.

Many individuals living with disabilities are not limited by their condition, but by inaccessible environments, limited awareness and barriers within systems. Through education, advocacy and accessibility awareness, InuAbility aims to contribute toward a more inclusive future.
