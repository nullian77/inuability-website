Designing With People, Not For People

Too often, accessibility is treated as something added later. True inclusion begins with listening to lived experience. "Nothing About Us Without Us" isn't just a slogan — it's fundamental to creating real change.

At InuAbility, we develop solutions with people — not just for them. When someone with a disability is in the room from day one, everything changes.
